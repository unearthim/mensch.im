To be a Mensch is to be more than just a person. It is to be a person of integrity, honor, and deep moral character. Someone you can count on.

It is a Yiddish word that has no perfect English translation, for it describes a quality of the soul. A quality of fundamental decency, of doing the right thing simply because it is the right thing to do.

This is the landmark for the brand that is built on character. For the ethical technology company, the B-Corp that puts people before profit, or the foundation dedicated to fostering a kinder, more honorable world.
